# Prompt Pack

## Master Character Prompt
Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: AI influencer character sheet and recurring social content
Primary request: create a photorealistic recurring AI influencer who looks like a regular metro Gen Z Indian girl living in Bangalore
Scene/backdrop: natural Bangalore urban environments such as metro stations, cafes, sidewalks, apartment balconies, bookstores, coworking spaces, and rainy streets
Subject: 22-24 year old Indian woman, medium brown skin, dark expressive eyes, dark slightly wavy hair, soft natural makeup, confident but approachable expression
Style/medium: realistic lifestyle photography, candid social-media aesthetic, not high-fashion editorial
Composition/framing: mostly handheld phone-camera framing, mirror selfies, waist-up portraits, cafe candids, walking shots, seated table shots
Lighting/mood: soft daylight, cloudy monsoon light, warm cafe light, evening city glow
Color palette: earthy neutrals, denim blue, muted green, warm beige, charcoal, soft maroon
Materials/textures: cotton shirts, denim, light gold jewelry, tote bags, metro interiors, rain reflections
Constraints: she must look believable, local, repeatable across many posts, and specifically Bangalore-coded rather than generic influencer; avoid luxury excess; avoid heavy glamour; avoid uncanny perfection
Avoid: doll-like face, plastic skin, exaggerated curves, fantasy styling, western party-club aesthetic, overly fair skin, studio fashion campaign look

## First Portrait Prompt
Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: reference portrait for social persona
Primary request: a photorealistic portrait of a Bangalore Gen Z Indian girl influencer for a recurring social media character
Scene/backdrop: outside a modern Bangalore cafe near a metro street, soft urban background blur, realistic Indian city details
Subject: 23 year old Indian woman, medium brown skin, oval face, expressive dark eyes, natural brows, black slightly wavy shoulder-length hair, subtle dewy makeup, small hoop earrings, simple chain, oversized light blue shirt over a white tank top, relaxed confident expression
Style/medium: realistic smartphone lifestyle photography
Composition/framing: vertical 4:5 portrait, chest-up framing, slight candid angle, looking just past the camera
Lighting/mood: soft overcast daylight, natural flattering shadows, calm and cool urban mood
Color palette: blue, white, grey, warm skin tones
Constraints: realistic Indian features, believable Bangalore Gen Z vibe, no visible text, no watermark, no uncanny skin smoothing
Avoid: model-like editorial glamour, perfect studio background, excessive makeup, western celebrity look
