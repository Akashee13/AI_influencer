# Shot Library

## Core Shot Types

### 1. Canonical Portrait
- chest-up
- soft daylight
- slight candid angle
- clean urban Bangalore backdrop
- use as identity anchor

### 2. Mirror Selfie
- apartment or cafe washroom mirror
- phone in hand
- casual fit
- believable room clutter

### 3. Metro Commute
- standing near doors
- tote bag or phone visible
- practical outfit
- candid commuter energy

### 4. Cafe Table
- coffee cup
- laptop or book
- window light
- mid-conversation or looking away

### 5. Street Walk
- Bangalore sidewalk
- traffic blur or signage ambiance
- tote bag, phone, light movement

### 6. Bookstore / Lifestyle Browsing
- browsing shelves
- warm indoor light
- soft candid expression

### 7. Rain Scene
- umbrella or wet pavement
- overcast tone
- city reflections

### 8. Balcony Golden Hour
- apartment balcony
- relaxed outfit
- urban skyline softness

## Reel Cover Types
- laughing metro frame
- coffee close-up with face in frame
- outfit-check mirror frame
- rain reaction frame

## Sequence Ideas For Video
- walk into metro station
- sit at cafe and look at phone
- fix hair and smile at camera
- hold coffee and glance sideways
- step out into rain and react

## Consistency Rule
Every new scene should preserve:
- same face
- same age band
- same skin tone
- same everyday Bangalore vibe

Only one major thing should change at a time:
- outfit
- location
- pose
- camera angle
